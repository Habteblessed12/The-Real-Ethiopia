YouTube Videos

Awash

Known for Awash National Park, this area features stunning waterfalls, wildlife, and volcanic landscapes. A perfect destination for nature lovers.

Wenchi

Wenchi Crater Lake offers peaceful scenery, hiking trails, and horseback riding around one of Ethiopia’s most beautiful volcanic lakes.

Arbaminch

Famous for the “Forty Springs” and Nechisar National Park, Arbaminch is rich in wildlife and offers views of Lakes Abaya and Chamo.

Hawassa

A vibrant lakeside city known for its relaxing atmosphere, fresh fish markets, and beautiful views of Lake Hawassa.
